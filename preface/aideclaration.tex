\chapter*{AI Tools Declaration}
\pagestyle{pre}

During the preparation of this thesis, AI tools (specifically ChatGPT and Claude by
Anthropic) were used to assist with grammar correction, clarity improvement and general
language refinement. The tools were applied conscientiously and in line with ethical
guidelines. No AI was used to generate original ideas, analyses, or references. All
research, analysis, interpretations, and conclusions are entirely the author's own, and
the author takes full responsibility for the content.
