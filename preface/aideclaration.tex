\chapter*{AI Tools Declaration}
\pagestyle{pre}

During the preparation of this thesis, AI tools, specifically ChatGPT and Claude by
Anthropic, were used in two ways. First, they were used for language support: grammar
correction, clarity improvement, and general language refinement. Second, they were used
as a sparring partner during the writing and structuring process, helping to think
through the organisation of arguments and the phrasing of technical explanations.
All original ideas, analyses, results, and conclusions are entirely the author's own.
No AI was used to generate references, analyse data, or produce research findings.
The author takes full responsibility for the content of this thesis.
